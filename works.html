Urban Mobility Concept

Autonomous urban transit concept

This project reimagines short-distance city transportation through a compact autonomous transit pod designed for fast and efficient urban mobility. Instead of traditional seating, the interior uses standing support fasteners and hanging grips, allowing passengers to travel comfortably for short durations while carrying bags or daily essentials.

The design focuses on maximizing passenger capacity, reducing congestion, and improving movement within dense city infrastructure. Its clean, minimal form language and seamless exterior create a futuristic yet approachable identity for next-generation smart transportation systems.

Teapoy Design

This teapoy design is inspired by the fluid and futuristic design language of Karim Rashid, reflecting his signature use of smooth curves, bold colours, and soft organic forms. The glossy finish and sculptural base create a modern, playful aesthetic while maintaining functional simplicity.

Biomimicry is expressed through the organic leg structure, which resembles the natural flow and growth patterns found in flower petals or plant roots. Instead of using rigid geometric supports, the design adopts smooth, nature-inspired curves that provide both visual balance and structural stability. The form gives the furniture a living, dynamic appearance, blending natural inspiration with contemporary furniture design.

Magic Table

Biomimicry Furniture Design

Magic table

This table design explores biomimicry through the organic form of an egg, translating its smooth curves and balanced structure into a functional furniture piece. The flowing continuous body reflects the natural strength and softness found in an eggshell, creating a form that feels stable yet visually lightweight. The open curved support beneath the tabletop enhances the sculptural appearance while maintaining structural balance and elegance.

The design language is strongly inspired by Karim Rashid, incorporating his signature characteristics of fluid geometry, minimalism, and futuristic aesthetics. The seamless surfaces, soft transitions, and bold contemporary form give the table a modern emotional identity, turning it from a simple utility object into a statement piece. The vibrant finish further emphasizes the playful and expressive nature of the design while maintaining a clean and sophisticated visual appeal.

Structural Validation

The structural analysis of the biomimicry-inspired table demonstrates how the continuous organic form efficiently distributes load across the curved body. The stress simulation highlights that most areas of the structure experience low stress levels, shown in blue and green, while the highest stress concentration occurs at the inner curved transition where the upper and lower surfaces connect. This indicates that the flowing eggshell-inspired geometry provides overall stability and strength while maintaining a lightweight sculptural appearance. The analysis validates the balance between aesthetics and structural performance within the design.

Oblivion Drone

Futuristic Combat Drone Concept

Oblivion Drone

Oblivion Drone is a futuristic aerial surveillance and combat drone concept developed to strengthen my CAD modeling and rendering workflow. The project focuses on creating a clean, aerodynamic form language inspired by sci-fi robotics and autonomous defense systems. The smooth outer shell contrasts with the exposed mechanical weapon modules, giving the drone a balance between minimalism and aggression.

The model was designed in CAD with attention to surface continuity, proportion, and detailing, while the final visualization was rendered in KeyShot Studio to explore realistic lighting, materials, and cinematic presentation.
