Oblivion Drone

Oblivion Drone is a futuristic aerial surveillance and combat drone concept developed to strengthen my CAD modeling and rendering workflow. The project focuses on creating a clean, aerodynamic form language inspired by sci-fi robotics and autonomous defense systems. The smooth outer shell contrasts with the exposed mechanical weapon modules, giving the drone a balance between minimalism and aggression.

The model was designed in CAD with attention to surface continuity, proportion, and detailing, while the final visualization was rendered in KeyShot Studio to explore realistic lighting, materials, and cinematic presentation. Through this project, I experimented with hard-surface modeling techniques, form development, and visual storytelling to create a believable next-generation autonomous drone concept.
