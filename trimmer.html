Nothing Trimmer Concept

A minimalist hair trimmer reimagined in Nothing's signature style. The matte white cylindrical body features a transparent mid-section that exposes the internal motor with subtle LED illumination — a direct nod to Nothing's iconic see-through aesthetic. A ridged adjustment dial, flush circular power button, and precision steel T-blade complete the look, while the subtle Nothing wordmark sits quietly near the base. Functional, beautiful, and unmistakably Nothing.
